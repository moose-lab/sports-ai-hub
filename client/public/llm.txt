## About

Sports AI Hub is the web app for the awesome-sports-ai ecosystem, a sports tools collection site with many AI tools, datasets, APIs, prototypes, and builder paths for sports services. It helps builders find practical building blocks for forecasting, athlete training workflows, match intelligence, scouting, video review, media generation, team operations, and fan experiences.

The hub is designed for open-source sports AI builders rather than only enterprise buyers. Key pages include the 2026 FIFA World Cup Match Center, which presents source-backed match data and links into the World Cup coverage contract and assistant toolkit.
